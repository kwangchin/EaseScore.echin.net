# Introduction

---

### Master Your Game with EaseScore: The Ultimate Scorekeeper

Ditch the mental math and stay focused on the kitchen! **EaseScore** is the intuitive, gesture-based scoring app designed for the modern pickleball player.

With a simple tap to score and a double tap to undo, tracking your match has never been easier. This brings professional-grade features to your wrist and pocket, including **smart server tracking** based on score parity, **real-time Apple Watch connectivity** status, and **localized voice announcements** in 10 different languages.

After the match, review your performance in the **Match History** or generate a **custom, shareable scorecard** to celebrate your win on social media. Whether youâ€™re playing a casual 11-point game or a competitive 21-point set, EaseScore ensures you never lose track of the score again.

## 24 Dec 2025, version 1.1

### Feature release!

#### Added features:
- Connectivity Indicator:
    - The Gear Icon now features a integrated "status light" in the top-right corner
    - Green: Apple Watch is Connected
    - Amber/Orange: Apple Watch is Disconnected
- Serving Indicator:
    - Winner Serves: The logic now assigns the serve to the team that just won the point
    - Parity-based Positioning:
        - If the server's score is Even: The ball icon appears to the Right of the score
        - If the server's score is Odd: The ball icon appears to the Left of the score
- Multi-Language Support:
    - Localization that supports for 10 languages (English, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Spanish, French, Portuguese, Russian, Indonesian, Japanese, Korean)
- Audio & Haptics:
    - Added "Game Over" and score announcement
    - Added "Sound Effects" and "Voice" toggles in Settings
    - Added language support for score annoucement
    - Added Haptics effect
- Match History
    - Added History View in Settings that lists past matches with Date, Scores and "Winner" crown icon
- Shareable Scorecard
    - Generate cool image of the final score, names with Pickleball icon
    - Added "Share" button to export the result image directly to Photos or share on Social Media

#### Fix & Improvements:
- Sync issues
